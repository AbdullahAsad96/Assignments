facts about Cats

Did you know that cats have an extra organ that allows them to taste scents?
Yes! AllCats have an extra organ that allows them to taste scents on the air, which is why your cat stares at you with her mouth open from time to time.
Cats have a unique grooming behavior called "allogrooming", where they groom each other as a form of social bonding.
Cats are nearsighted, but their peripheral vision and night vision are much better than that of humans
